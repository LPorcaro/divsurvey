Frequently Asked Questions

  • During the survey, do I have to listen to the tracks entirely?

    Not necessarily. Feel free to go forward and backward in the lists, behaving like you normally do when you listen to music.

  • How have you selected the tracks and artists shown in the survey?

    We surfed the web looking for electronic music artists from all around the world.
